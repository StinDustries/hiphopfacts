Hip Hop History Month Facts

Hip Hop History Fact 3: Hip Hop as a genre is male-dominated. In fact, when asked to compose a list of female rapper, a group of 20 8th graders came up with a list of 8: Nicki Minaj, Lil Kim, Cardi B, Salt-n-pepa, Iggy Azalea, Queen Latifah, and Rhianna. This is also shown in genres such as metal and rock.
