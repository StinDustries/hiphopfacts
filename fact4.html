Hip Hop History Month Facts

Hip Hop History Fact 4: A very interesting thing about the duo MC Rob Base/DJ E-Z Rock is that the DJ gets billing in the artist name, something that does not usually happen in modern hip-hop bands. Even during the beginning of hip-hop, groups without the DJ in the artist billing would make songs "featuring" the DJ. One group (Run-DMC) even wrote a whole song about how awesome their DJ was, and named the song after him. These are things that wouldn't happen in the modern hip-hop community.
