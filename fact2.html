Hip Hop History Month Facts

Hip Hop History Fact 2: Hip Hop as a movement started in the South Bronx when a man by the name of DJ Kool Herc used a new turntable technique to extend the lyric breaks in between songs. This is also where the modern term "breakdancing" came from
